TIMELINE

  • 1975. He and Allen founded Microsoft in Albuquerque, New Mexico.

  • Since 1987. Gates has been included in the Forbes list of the world's wealthiest people.

  • June 2008. Gates transitioned to a part-time role at Microsoft and full-time work at the Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation.

  • February 2014. He stepped down as chairman of the board of Microsoft and assumed a new post as technology adviser to support the newly appointed CEO Satya Nadella.
